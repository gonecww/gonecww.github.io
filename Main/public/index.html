What you get

Built for creators who care about quality

Codec-aware encoding

Choose H.264 for compatibility or H.265/HEVC for superior compression at equivalent quality. Both tuned for TikTok's ingest pipeline.

Resolution presets

Optimized presets for 1080×1920, 720×1280 and custom resolutions. Maintains aspect ratio and avoids upscaling artifacts.

Frame rate preservation

Lock your source frame rate or normalize to 30/60fps. Prevents judder and duplicate frames that TikTok's encoder amplifies.

Audio quality control

AAC-LC at up to 320kbps. Normalize loudness to -14 LUFS so your audio sounds identical after TikTok's own normalization pass.

Bitrate optimizer

CRF + maxrate capping prevents the file from going over TikTok's preferred ingest ceiling while maximizing visual fidelity.

Secure by design

Files are validated server-side, isolated per session, and automatically purged after download. Nothing lingers on our servers.
